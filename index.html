About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer, Audio Engineer, Musician, and all around great guy.

People call me a "Hipster", but I don't like labels.

Born and Raised in Chicago, I grew up with a broad exposure to music from my siblings. This lead me to starting a career as a Audio Engineer, where I worked in recording studios.

After freelancing for five years, I decided to pursue my passion for Web Development. I attended General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive program from March to June of 2015. Here I learned the skills to not only build what you can see here, but the ability to continue learning, and adapting to the constantly changing industry of web development. Some might say it’s like.......The Wild Wild West.
